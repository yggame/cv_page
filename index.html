Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Department of Electronic Engineering and Information Science, School of Information Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China.(USTC)), and my supervisors are Professor Xun Chen of X-Lab and Associate Professor Aiping Liu.

My research area is mainly medical image processing, including low-level and high-level tasks, such as Super-resolution, Synthesis, Segmentation, etc.
